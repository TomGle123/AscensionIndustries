Customize Your Pants

For security reasons, the relic will always look somewhat unassuming. However, you can choose from several aesthetic options.

Below are some of the available styles and their corresponding activation cantrips. NOTE: You can only change the relic's appearance once!

Classic White Underpants

The understated look for the traditionalist. Simple, timeless, and reliable.

Cantrip: "Vestis Albus Eternum"

Tight Ring

For those who like the snug feel of a ring on their digit. Elegant and discreet.

Cantrip: "Annulus Vinculum Ascendit"

Earring

Ascend and look fabulous. A bold choice for the daring adventurer. (Right side only)

Cantrip: "Ornamentum Aureum Elevare"

Belt

Channel the power of accessories and make any outfit pop with a splash of color.

Cantrip: "Cingulum Virtutis Volare"
