Tailoring & Runes

  • Rune Pockets: Sew on rune-lined pockets to store tiny reagents.
  • Lengthening: Visit any reputable tailor or use a Minor Ward to adjust fit.
  • Dyes: Use stable dyes; avoid moonflower dye during full moons.

Crafting Add-ons

Attach a toggle for manual ascent triggers, or inscribe quips into the hem for roleplay bonuses.
