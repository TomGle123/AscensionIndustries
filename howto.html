Quick Start

  1. Don the pants with intent — focus on a destination (roof, balcony, or ally's dramatic reveal).
  2. On your turn, declare the Ascend action to the DM and roll a charisma check (DC as agreed).
  3. When successful, levitate up to 10 feet per success tier for 1d4 rounds; descending is free.

Advanced Techniques

Combine with a gust of wind cantrip for extended floatation. Do not attempt with feathers and too much confidence.
