Origin Story

Forged by a disgraced wizard with a flair for dramatics, the Pants of Ascension were originally meant for stage magicians.

FAQ

Can I wear them during combat?
Yes, but levitation may draw unwanted attention. Use tactically.
How do I wash them?
Hand wash with mild soap; avoid holy water unless instructed by a cleric.
